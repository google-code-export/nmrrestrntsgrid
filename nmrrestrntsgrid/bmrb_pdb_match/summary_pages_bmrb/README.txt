This directory contains the results from the script:
doInternal_method_linking.csh.

The data is used for linking from BMRB entries to PDB entries for the BMRB
summary pages.

bmrb_2_nmr_restraints_grid_link.csv
    The links between BMRB entries and PDB entries in the NMR Restraints Grid.
bmrb_2_pdb_for_pdb_image_all.csv
    The links between BMRB entries and PDB entries.
bmrb_2_pdb_for_pdb_image_only.csv
    The links between BMRB entries and PDB entries NOT in the Grid.
